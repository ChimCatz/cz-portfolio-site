Enjoyment April 13, 2026

The Faithful Witness of God for the Testimony of Jesus

To experience the golden lampstand as the testimony of Jesus, which is the corporate expression of Jesus, we must be those who are filled with the Spirit of Jesus by continually calling on the name of the Lord Jesus and who bear the brands of Jesus on our bodies; everything that happens in our circumstances is for the producing of the beaten lampstand.

Our service today in time is a preparation for our service in eternity; God's unique goal in time is to dispense Himself into us day by day; only when God comes in and God goes out of us do we have a service.
